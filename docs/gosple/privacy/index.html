Privacy Policy

Firmware Foundation Studios, a brand of The QB Stable LLC (Tampa, FL)

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

This privacy policy covers all apps published by Firmware Foundation Studios, including Gosple and any future titles. We built games, not data collection tools. Your privacy is fully intact when you use our apps.

What Data We Collect

None. Our apps do not ask for your name, email, location, or any other personal information. There is no account creation. There is no login. You download the app and you play.

What Is Stored on Your Device

Our apps save your game progress locally on your device so you can pick up where you left off. This may include:

  • Current streak and scores
  • Badges or achievements earned
  • Puzzle or level completion history
  • Player profile selections

This data lives only on your device using local storage. It is never sent to our servers or anywhere else. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, advertising networks, or data processors. No SDKs phone home. No pixels fire. Your activity in the app stays in the app.

Advertising

Our apps are paid downloads with no ads. We do not show ads and we do not share data with advertisers. If a future title includes ads for our own games within the app, no personal data will be collected or shared in the process.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are rated 4+ and are safe for children of all ages. Because we collect absolutely no data from anyone, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). There is nothing to collect, nothing to share, and nothing to worry about.

Parents and guardians can feel confident letting their children use our apps without any privacy concerns.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with anyone. This is straightforward because we do not have any user data to share in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with a new effective date. Since we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly. We recommend checking this page occasionally.

If we ever change our approach to data collection, we will update this policy before making any changes.
