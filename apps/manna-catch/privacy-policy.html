Manna Catch Privacy Policy

Published by Firmware Foundation Studios, a brand of The QB Stable LLC (Tampa, FL)

Effective Date: May 28, 2026

The Short Version

Manna Catch does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We built a Bible catching game, not a data collection tool. Your privacy is fully intact when you use this app.

What Data We Collect

None. Zero. Manna Catch does not ask for your name, email, location, or any other personal information. There is no account creation. There is no login. You download the app, and you play.

What Is Stored on Your Device

Manna Catch saves your game progress locally on your device so you can pick up where you left off. This includes:

  • Your current streak
  • Badges you have earned
  • Game score history

This data lives only on your device using local storage. It is never sent to our servers or anywhere else. If you delete the app, this data is deleted with it.

Third-Party Services

Manna Catch does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, advertising networks, or data processors. No SDKs phone home. No pixels fire. Your activity in the app stays in the app.

Advertising

There are no ads in Manna Catch. The app is a free download, and that is the entire business model. We do not show ads, and we do not share data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Manna Catch is rated 4+ and is safe for children of all ages. Because we collect absolutely no data from anyone, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). There is nothing to collect, nothing to share, and nothing to worry about.

Parents and guardians can feel confident letting their children use Manna Catch without any privacy concerns.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with anyone. This is straightforward because we do not have any user data to share in the first place.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted here with a new effective date. Since we do not collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly. We recommend checking this page occasionally.

If we ever change our approach to data collection (we have no plans to), we will update this policy before making any changes.
