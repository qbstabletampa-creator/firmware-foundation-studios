# Firmware Foundation Studios Wiki Schema

This vault tracks CJ's Christian kids game studio, app factory, product decisions, notes, research, build specs, charity decisions, and release plans.

## Page Format

Every page uses:

```markdown
# Page Title

**Summary:** One paragraph overview.
**Last Updated:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Sources:** Conversation, research links, files, or agent notes.
**Related:** [[related-page]]

---

## Notes
```

## Rules

- Record every product decision here.
- Keep the factory strategy permanent: many small standalone apps, one reusable template, scale winners.
- One page per major concept.
- Update index.md and log.md after changes.
- Use public Christian, parent trusted language.
- No ads, no chat, no child social features.
- Do not use protected likenesses, actor names, or show names in public prompts.
