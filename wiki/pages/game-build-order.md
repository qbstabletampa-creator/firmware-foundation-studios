## First Wave

1. Gosple
2. Manna Catch
3. Noah Animal Match

5. Bible Brick Breaker

## Second Wave

1. Ark Hopper
2. David Sling Shot
3. Shepherd Maze
4. Little Light Runner
5. Parable Pinball

## Last Wave

World, Roblox, and Minecraft style games come later.

Examples:

- Little Light World
- Build Eden
- Bible Obby Adventures
- Kingdom Journey
- Little Light Quest

## Decision

Start with simple replayable arcade and puzzle loops.

Do not start with a giant world app.
