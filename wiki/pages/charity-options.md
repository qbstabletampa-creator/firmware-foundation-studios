# Charity Options

**Summary:** Locked month to month tithe partners for Firmware Foundation Studios.
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-27
**Sources:** Charity Navigator search results, ECFA, organization pages.
**Related:** [[studio-decisions]], [[gosple-product-spec]]

---

## Recommendation

CJ locked **Awana** plus **Hope Children's Home, Tampa** as the month to month rotation.

Current giveback model:

- 10 percent tithe from app revenue
- Month A: Awana
- Month B: Hope Children's Home, Tampa

Backup shortlist if the rotation ever expands:

1. Awana
2. Hope Children's Home, Tampa
3. Compassion International
4. Child Evangelism Fellowship
5. Children's Hunger Fund

## Options

### Awana

Why it fits:
- Direct children's ministry and discipleship
- Works through churches
- Strong match for a Christian kids app studio
- Charity Navigator result shows 4 of 4 stars
- ECFA profile says Awana reaches millions of kids and youth globally

Best angle:

> 10 percent supports kids learning the gospel and growing in lifelong discipleship.

Sources:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/362428692
- https://www.ecfa.org/MemberProfile.aspx?ID=6649

### Compassion International

Why it fits:
- Christian child development ministry
- Helps children in poverty in Jesus' name
- Partners with local churches globally
- Charity Navigator profile confirms mission and scale
- Strong public trust and name recognition

Best angle:

> 10 percent supports children facing poverty through local church partners.

Source:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/362423707

### Child Evangelism Fellowship

Why it fits:
- Direct gospel ministry to children
- Has Florida presence
- Charity Navigator result shows 4 of 4 stars
- Strong fit with public Christian positioning

Watchout:
- More explicitly evangelistic, which is good if we want bold Christian alignment, but may be less broad than child development or hunger relief.

Sources:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/386091187
- https://www.cefflorida.com/

### Hope Children's Home, Tampa

Why it fits:
- Local Tampa connection
- Christian home for children
- Charity Navigator result shows 4 of 4 stars
- Strong local story CJ can stand behind

Best angle:

> 10 percent supports a Florida Christian home serving children who need stability, care, and family structure.

Sources:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/620879012
- https://hopechildrenshome.org/florida/

### Children's Hunger Fund

Why it fits:
- Christian charity focused on children and families
- Charity Navigator result shows 4 of 4 stars
- Search result showed 99 percent Charity Navigator score and high program efficiency

Best angle:

> 10 percent helps deliver food and hope to children and families.

Sources:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/954335462

### Convoy of Hope

Why it fits:
- Faith based relief organization
- Children's feeding initiatives
- Charity Navigator result shows long track record of 4 star ratings

Watchout:
- Broader disaster relief and feeding ministry, not only kids ministry.

Sources:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/680051386
- https://convoyofhope.org/convoy-news/convoy-recognized-for-consistently-receiving-charity-navigators-highest-award/

### World Vision

Why it fits:
- Christian humanitarian organization serving children, families, and communities
- Charity Navigator result shows 4 of 4 stars and 94 percent score
- Strong name recognition

Watchout:
- Large organization, less personal and less specifically kids ministry than Awana, CEF, or Hope Children's Home.

Sources:
- https://www.charitynavigator.org/ein/951922279

## Locked Pick

Use **Awana** as Month A.

Use **Hope Children's Home, Tampa** as Month B.

Keep **Compassion International**, **Child Evangelism Fellowship**, and **Children's Hunger Fund** as backups only.
